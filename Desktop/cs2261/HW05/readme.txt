Cooking Mama Dish Stacking Game!

Press start to being the game. Move mama by using the left and right arrow keys. Try to collect all of the blue dishes. You must collect at least 8 dishes to win and make mama happy! If you collect a bad dish (the red ones), it's okay, you can collect up to 3 bad dishes and still win. If you collect more than 3 bad dishes, you lose and mama will be angry! To pause the game, press start.
